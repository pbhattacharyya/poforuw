Sunbeam

A peer-to-peer philanthropy platform to benefit victims of recent natural disasters.

process

interviews -> persona development -> competitive analysis -> storyboarding & speed-dating -> needs validation -> low-fidelity prototyping -> concept validation -> design system creation -> high-fidelity prototyping

team

Nishchala Singhal, Yulin Liu

duration

2017 / 6 months

Problem Statement

Many victims of natural disasters find themselves strapped for cash in their time of need. There are a number of organizations that provide relief to these victims, but the relief might take days to get there. Those few days are crucial, and small amounts of money can go a long way toward helping victims secure essential items such as food, shelter, and medicine.

Outcome

Sunbeam is a peer-to-peer philanthropy plaftorm that allows people from the around the world to make direct donations to the victims of a recent natural disaster. The product has two key users. Beneficiaries are the victims of a recent natural disaster. They use Sunbeam to seek monetary assistance to meet urgent needs. Donors are the well-wishers who want to help others out of a tight spot during a crisis.
